About Us



Who We Are

We are rising juniors at West Windsor - Plainsboro High School South and we aspire to develop business skills by running our own organization. At the same time, we want to teach subjects that we love to other students so that they may share a passion for the subject. Furthermore, we feel that our organization will better prepare students for high school because we are currently going through high school and have a better understanding of the curriculum, expectations, and standards that these ambitious students will eventually face.

Mission Statement

Our goals are to educate and advance children to better understand basic academic concepts so they can achieve better results in school.
